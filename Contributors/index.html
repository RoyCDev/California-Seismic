Meet the Team

Summer 2020

California Seismic is brought to you by a group of determined students and recent graduates who believe in the mission of promoting access to education and giving back to the civil engineering society. Our members represent the diversity and strength of civil engineering, and their work has enabled future engineers to study without barriers.
