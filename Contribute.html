General Contact Information

Submit your name and email if you would like to be contacted to create/curate resources. Our end goal is to make studying for the Seismic Exam easier, and we can do it with your help.

Practice Question Submittal

If you woud like to contribute a question that you've created, please use the below form. Questions will be reviewed and checked for any likeness to other popular question resources. If the question appears to be a direct copy of another author's work, or a copy with word changes or number changes, then the question will be discarded and not used on this website. We will always err on the side of caution when evaluating user-generated questions.
