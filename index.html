Who We Are

Pictured: Doe Library, University of California, Berkeley

California Seismic is curated by a group of young professionals who want to make structural engineering a better profession. Our friendship began at UC Berkeley, where we learned that people succeed through open collaboration. In the spirit of collaboration, resources have been created and freely provided to make the Seismic Principles Exam easier.

There currently exist no free comprehensive resource for the Seismic Principles Exam. There are many expensive alternatives, but putting a price on culminated knowledge is not in the spirit of structural engineering.

We hope you find these resources helpful, and wish you good luck on your studies.
