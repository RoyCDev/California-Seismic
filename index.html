About Us

We are a group of friends working to make structural engineering a better profession. Progress is made in our industry when information is freely shared, so we wanted to extend our structural engineering knowledge to anyone who is studying for seismic exam. There exists numerous costly resources for the seismic exam, but that is not the spirit of the structural engineering profession. Our hope is by making these resources freely available to engineers of all background, that they will pass the exam cheaper and faster, giving them more time to give back to their own profession.

Our mission: To provide EIT's with all necessary study materials to successfuly pass the CA Seismic Exam, earn their PE license, and contribute to a great profession.
